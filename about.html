About Brandon Guo

I am a junior at MVHS and live in Saratoga, CA. My hobbies come and go; currently, I like to ride my bike, run, and play chess. I am an avid listener of classical (orchestral and solo piano) music, though I seldom perform anymore, other than occasionally with my local orchestra. I also enjoy playing soccer with De Anza Force and my school team, so much so that I dedicate a webpage to it.

In ten years' time, I would like to pursue an advanced degree in statistics and become a quantitative analyst. Much of what appears on my (current) resume intends to reflect such goals of mine. But of course, I am still a high schooler, so I try to keep an open mind.
